What is this?

StudentLink is a student focused social platform built for universities.

Its the ultimate way to stay in touch with classmates, get used supplies, and hear the latest campus news!


What can I do?

StudentLink allows you to make posts, buy or sell items, and connect with your campus hub!

You can also add friends and protect yourself by blocking users or making your account private.


What data do you collect?

StudentLink only collects your email address, name, posts, listings, comments, and friends you make on the site.

These, however, are not given to any 3rd parties and are collected only to support app functionality.

You are free to permanently delete your account and its data at any time.


I came across disturbing or inappropriate content. What should I do?

StudentLink allows you to report posts to administrators who will review the post and your reason for the report.

If the report is deemed valid, the post will be removed and the offending user may receive restrictions or a ban.

In the event that a report is not deemed valid, it is suggested that you block further interactions with the user to give yourself the best experience.

You can see your reports and blocked users in Settings at any time.
